Hi, I am Haidak

I am a Researcher at Zhejiang Normal University (ZJNU) at the Department of Computer Sciences and Technology.

My research is mainly based on analytical, theoretical and practical analysis. My PhD program is focused on building New Dynamic Software capable of predicting the behaviour or lubricating interface in Axial Piston Machines, which are used in Aviation (Mobile Robot), agriculture (excavator) and Construction.

On this website, you can find some of my publications, International Conferences, Research Guides, and some data visualization projects. My resume can also be downloaded at this link. at this link.

I am organizing Online Meetings and Conferences which can help people to interact and share research ideas. If you are a researcher who need to talk and discuss to other people, if you are interested on sharing your ideas or have some issues on leading your research, this is the right place.
